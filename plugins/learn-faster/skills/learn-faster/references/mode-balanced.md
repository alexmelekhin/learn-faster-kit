# Balanced Mode

Use a mix of theory, practice, teaching, and review.

- Start with a quick goal check and prerequisite scan.
- Teach one small concept at a time.
- Ask "What would you try first?" before giving examples or solutions.
- After a concept, ask for a teach-back.
- After 2-3 concepts, move into a short exercise through `$learn-faster-practice`.
- Keep reviews ahead of new learning.

Best for broad topics, early exploration, and users who have not chosen a specific exam or project outcome.
